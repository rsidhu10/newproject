The scheme template consists of the following fields:


Column Name Column Description
Number Serial or row number
Scheme Name The name of the scheme
Scheme Type Type of Scheme: TW , HP
Operated By Who the scheme is operated by: GP, DWSS, GPWSC
D_LPCD The designed LPCD capacity of the scheme
Operational Status The operational status of the scheme: Operational, Running, Work In Progress, Bore Fail
